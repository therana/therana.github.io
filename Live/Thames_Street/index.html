    All our products maintain a high qulity resortwear standard. You can find something for the whole family with sizes for children, men and women with the stylish and cool design that lasts a lifetime.

    Superior Comfort Unisex Resortwear

    Our mission at Thames Street Shirt Company is simple. We aim to be the ultimate provider of high quality resortwear basics with lasting style and comfort. Shopping men, women, or kids is easy and convenient.

    • T-Shirts

      Come in to Thames Street Shirt Co and find the right mix of style and comfort. Our shirts are made with high quality materials, and all designs are Newport original

    • Sweatshirts

      Our sweatshirts range from pullover hoodies to plain pullover and zip ups. Find sweatshirts for the whole family in a variety of designs and colors.

    • Jackets

      Step in and find our selection of nautical themed rain jackets, winter jackets, and windbreaker style jackets. Jackets come embroidered or printed and we can handle custom requests for corporate or private parties.

    • Childrens Clothing

      Get the little ones something they can grow comfortable in. We have multiple styles from the newborns to pre-teen and everything in between.

    • Sweatpants

      Complete the casualwear with sweatpants offering a variety of nautical designs and styles. Enjoy the high quality fabric used :-)

    • Accessories

      Beyond the shirts and pants we've got a variety of accessories like umbrellas, magnets, shoes and more. Stop in to explore what we've got as it's always changing!
