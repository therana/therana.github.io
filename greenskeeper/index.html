Dating back to 1988, Greenskeeper Inc. has been designing and building landscape projects, adding both beauty and value to homes and commercial properties. With a degree in agricultural plant & soil science from the University of Rhode Island, President John Ferrucci has the knowledge and experience to handle any size project.
